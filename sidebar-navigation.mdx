Nobori.ai is organized as **Track → Measure → Act**.  
Use global filters at the top (Tags, Country, Date range, Platforms) to scope all views.

## Global Filters
- **Tags** — optional prompt grouping  
- **Country** — market to read/run  
- **Date range** — 7/30 days or custom  
- **Platforms** — limit to specific AI models

<Note>Filters apply to Dashboard, Prompts, Competitors, and Citations.</Note>

## Track

### Dashboard
Overview of performance.
- **Visibility Score** — % of prompts where your brand appears, over time
- **Competitors** — rank table by visibility
- **Cited Domains** — domains AI relies on most

### Prompts
Manage and analyze prompts.
- Tabs: **Active**, **Suggested**, **Inactive**
- Actions: **Add Prompt**, **Export**, bulk Activate/Deactivate/Delete
- **Prompt Details**:
  - visibility chart with competitor toggle
  - **Executions** — AI responses (status, platform, response link, mentions)
  - **Citations** — URLs used with columns: *Used*, *Platforms*, *Type* (Publisher/Owned/Competitor/Community/Other), *Mentions*, *Mentioned*

### Competitors
Add and benchmark brands.
- Tabs: **Active**, **Suggested** (reject/activate)
- Actions: **Add Competitor** (Name, Website), **Export**, **Delete**
- Table shows **Visibility** (sentiment not live)

## Measure

### Citations
Everything AI models cite to answer your prompts.
- Tabs:
  - **Cited Pages** — URL-level list (Used count/%, Platforms, Type, Mentions, Mentioned)
  - **Cited Domains** — domain-level summary
- Use to identify publishers to contact and owned pages to strengthen

<Note>Mentions (brand named) and Citations (source used) are different signals.</Note>

## Act

### Get Cited
Operational outreach workspace.
- **Contact Publishers** — AI-cited pages missing your brand
- Actions: **Draft Email**, status pipeline (**Needs Attention → In Progress → Completed → Canceled**)

## Workspace

### Plans & Pricing
Plan, usage limits (Prompts / Runs / Action Credits), upgrades.

### Support
Contact support.

### Settings
Tabs:
- **Company** — company info, members & roles
- **Brand** — brand name, domain, logo; platforms (ChatGPT, AI Overviews, Perplexity), refresh frequency (3 days / 24 hours), primary country (coming soon)
- **Account** — profile and appearance (language/locale coming soon)
- **Billing** — Stripe billing & invoices
- **Notifications** — placeholder
- **Integrations** — coming soon
