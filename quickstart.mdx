# Quickstart

The fastest way to see how your brand appears across generative AI platforms.  
**Nobori.ai** measures your visibility across ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews — showing real data on **Mentions**, **Citations**, and **Sentiment**.

After completing this guide, you’ll have your first visibility results on the **Dashboard**.

---

## Overview

Nobori.ai collects authentic visibility data by simulating real user interactions with AI tools.  
This gives you measurable insights into how AI models reference your brand, your content, and your competitors.

<Note>
You’ll start by setting up your project, adding prompts, and reviewing your first visibility data — all within a few minutes.
</Note>

<Accordion title="Step 1: Set Up Your Project" icon="settings">

Your **project** defines what Nobori.ai will track — your brand, competitors, and visibility targets.

**In Project Settings:**

| Field | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Brand Name** | Exact brand name to look for in AI responses. |
| **Domain** | Your main website domain (used for source detection). |
| **Location / IP Source** | Choose the market or region where prompts run. |
| **Models** | Select AI models to monitor (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, etc.). |

<Note>
Your brand will also appear under *Competitors* — this allows benchmarking against your own visibility as part of the analysis.
</Note>

</Accordion>

---

<Accordion title="Step 2: Create Prompts" icon="list">

Prompts are the real questions users type into AI platforms — they generate your visibility data.

Examples:
- What is the best CRM for SaaS companies?  
- Which analytics tools are trusted by startups?  
- What platforms help track brand visibility in AI?

**How to start:**
- **Use Suggestions:** Nobori.ai generates prompts automatically based on your site and category.  
- **Add Manually:** Go to the *Prompts* section → click **Add Prompt** → write your own questions.

<Note>
Start with 3–5 prompts where you’d expect your brand to appear.
</Note>

</Accordion>

---

<Accordion title="Step 3: Review Your Dashboard" icon="chart-bar">

After your prompts are created, Nobori.ai runs daily AI queries and compiles your first visibility data.

You’ll see:
- **Mentions** — where your brand is named in AI answers  
- **Citations** — where your content or site is referenced
